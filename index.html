Introduction

ATAH – Intimacy with God Ministry is a God ordained movement birthed with one core mandate: to draw people into deep, personal, and unbroken communion with the Father.

The name ATAH, meaning Intimacy with God, reflects our conviction that true transformation flows from closeness with Jesus — not religion, not routine, but relationship.

Intimacy With God

We are committed to raising a generation whose greatest pursuit is the Presence of God. Through worship gatherings, spiritual impartation, mentorship, and kingdom- focused initiatives, ATAH creates an atmosphere where hearts are healed, destinies rekindled, and believers awakened to their identity in Christ.

What Guides Us?

Our foundational scripture is Psalm 27:4 “One thing I ask of the Lord... that I may dwell in the house of the Lord all the days of my life.” This is the heartbeat of ATAH: a single desire for God above all else

Since inception, ATAH has grown into a ministry known for:

  • Spirit-led worship encounters
  • Revival of personal devotion
  • Empowering ministers and worshippers
  • Restoring intimacy in the Body of Christ
  • Raising disciples hungry for God’s purpose

Our Mission, Vision & Confession

Our Mission

To create platforms where worship becomes a lifestyle and believers are equipped to walk closely with the Holy Spirit daily.

Our Vision

To build an end-time army that passionately pursues God, carries His presence, and transforms nations through intimacy and obedience.

Our Confession

ATAH A sound of revival. A call to deeper fellowship. A ministry revealing the heart of God.
